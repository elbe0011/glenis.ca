What is a Glenis?

I solve problems while always keeping clarity and simplicity in mind, and I love to use motion to move people. Who doesn’t like it when things end up all neat and pretty? I believe that the world just works better when we really pay attention to the small details and try to introduce elegance in what we make and what we do. My ma' and pa' love to bicker but they've always agreed on one thing: "Keeping things clean keeps things easy."
